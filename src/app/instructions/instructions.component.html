How to Use My App

Welcome to My App! Follow these instructions to make the most out of your experience:

  1. Create a Person:

    You can create a new person by clicking the Editor button and then clicking on the "Create Person" button. Provide the necessary information and submit the form.

  2. Create a Task:

    Similarly, create a new task, click on the "Create Task" button. Fill in the details and click "Submit."

  3. Edit or Delete:

    To edit or delete a task or person, click on the corresponding buttons. Confirm deletions through the confirmation dialog.

  4. View All Tasks and Persons:

    Explore all your tasks and persons by navigating to the respective sections. Use the delete and edit buttons to manage your data.

  5. Refresh Data:

    For the latest data, you can refresh the tasks and persons by clicking the refresh button in their respective sections.

  6. Connect with Me:

    Visit the "About Me" section to learn more about me and connect on social platforms, including LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, GitHub, and Discord.
